Privacy Policy for WireDesk

Effective Date: June 8, 2026

WireDesk respects your privacy and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

WireDesk does not collect any information from you. Specifically:

  • No user data is transmitted to any servers.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No third-party services or SDKs are integrated.
  • All app data remains on your device.

Local Data

All documents, records, settings, preferences, and app-related data in WireDesk are stored locally on your device. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible by the developer.

Permissions

WireDesk may request access to local device features strictly to support core functionality. These permissions do not involve any data collection or tracking.

Third-Party Sharing

No information is shared with third parties. No internet connection is required to use WireDesk.

Contact

This app does not collect or process any personal data. No contact is required.
