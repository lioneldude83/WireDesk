Privacy Policy for WireDesk

Effective Date: June 19, 2026

WireDesk respects your privacy. It does not collect or transmit personal data; app settings, bookmarks, and cache data remain local to your Mac.

Data Collection

WireDesk does not collect any information from you. Specifically:

  • No user data is transmitted to any servers.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No third-party services or SDKs are integrated.
  • All app data remains on your device.

Local Data

All documents, records, settings, preferences, and app-related cache data in WireDesk are stored locally on your Mac. This information never leaves your Mac and is not accessible by the developer.

Permissions

WireDesk may ask you to choose folders, camera-card sources, or destination folders so it can browse, import, and save files you select. These permissions are used only for local file access and do not involve data collection or tracking.

Third-Party Sharing

No information is shared with third parties.
